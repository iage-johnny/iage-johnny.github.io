We also have a broad section for local business to place classified advertisement or information and for business owners or buyers to place classified advertisement at business selling / buying, recruitments, property leasing/selling, etc., anything regarding to business matter, you are able to find information in business weekly. Property Weekly

This is the only dedicated property weekly in Chinese available in South Australia. It provides the latest issues of real estate and property listings in SA, including property related featured coloums, weekly real estate news update.

As time goes by, it has become the buying and selling platform for property buyer/seller as well as working as a pathway for local real estate agencies to merge in and connect with the Chinese community in SA.

Property Weekly is specifically designed to target the real estate market in Chinese Community. Also we also cover a big range of related industry information including, mortgage, development, interior design, property legal advises and so on.
