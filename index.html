HELLO AND WELCOME

Adelaide's Leading Chinese Media Group


Established in 2007, I Age Media Group is the pioneer and the most outstanding company in South Australia Chinese media industry. Over the past years, we have proudly developed and created many remarkable benchmarks into the Chinese media market.

To provide independent and high quality service, we believe that sincerity, communication and diligence are the foundation of our company working culture.


Yes, we are unique and standing out from the crowd.

Why?


Because we are the first Chinese media group in South Australia to exercise and follow the multi-channel and multi-media marketing and branding approaches.

I Age Media Group of companies include I Age Media, the media publisher focus on building variety of media channels and social platforms; and iAge Creative, the marketing and advertising agency focus on strategy planning and clients promotional services.

I AGE MEDIA

I AGE MEDIA

We love publishing and we do well


I Age media has established a strong network connection with the SA Chinese community as well as the local mainstream Australia community since 2007.

There are 3 Chinese weekly newspapers fave been established and been publishing under I Age Media. The general weekly news - I Age Weekly; the weekly business news - Business Weekly; and the weekly investment and property news - Property Weekly.

As the world has reached to a digital and mobile generation, I Age Media constantly adapting and absorbing new technology. And we have developed online and social media channels to ensure we can reach to all demography.

Our local Chinese social media account “TodayAdelaide” on Wechat and Weibo covers local SA news and information; and our news portals www.sachinese.com covers even more news and updates from Australia wide and international wide.
